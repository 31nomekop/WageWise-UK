Privacy summary

WageWise UK runs in your browser. Your inputs are processed on your device to calculate estimates.

Data collection

WageWise UK does not ask you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

Local storage

If you use features such as saved scenarios, they are stored locally on your device (in your browser storage) and are not uploaded anywhere.

Changes

If privacy-related behaviour changes in a future update, this page will be updated to reflect that.
